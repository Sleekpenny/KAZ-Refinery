Crude oil production by Kaz Refinery Oil (including share of production by equity affiliates) in 2019 totalled 96.645 million tones (1.953 million barrels per day), of which 64.147 million tonnes were produced by Company subsidiaries.

A total of 193 new production wells were commissioned, including 109 horizontal wells. Average daily flow at new wells was 37.8 tonnes, including 65.5 tonnes from horizontal wells. Total production from new wells (Group share) was 5.47 million tonnes. Drilling of horizontal wells has been found to be highly efficient and the Company plans to increase their numbers in the medium term. We lead the development of Kaz Refinery Oil's strategy, to drive the long-term resilience and value of our business and create value for our stakeholders more widely, including society, our employees and our shareholders. Processing products is for us one of the most budget-oriented activities.
